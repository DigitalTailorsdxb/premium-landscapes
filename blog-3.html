Artificial Grass vs Natural Turf: Which is Right for Your UK Garden?

January 2025 • 6 min read

Choosing between artificial grass and natural turf? This comprehensive guide compares costs, maintenance, durability, and aesthetics to help you make the right decision for your UK garden.

Initial Cost Comparison

Natural Turf

£10-£18 per m²

Including preparation and installation

Artificial Grass

£48-£96 per m²

Including sub-base and installation

Maintenance Requirements

Natural Turf

  • Weekly mowing (spring-autumn)
  • Regular watering
  • Seasonal feeding & treatment
  • Weed control
  • Annual scarifying

Artificial Grass

  • Occasional brushing
  • Periodic rinsing
  • Remove debris
  • No mowing, watering, or feeding

Long-Term Cost Analysis

Over a 10-year period, natural turf maintenance costs (mowing, treatments, water) typically total £1,800-£3,600. Artificial grass requires minimal maintenance, making it more cost-effective long-term despite higher upfront costs.

Aesthetics & Feel

Modern artificial grass looks remarkably realistic, but natural turf still offers unmatched authenticity. Natural grass feels softer underfoot and has that distinctive fresh-cut aroma. However, artificial grass stays green year-round, even during dry UK summers or wet winters.

Durability & Lifespan

  • Natural Turf: Can last indefinitely with proper care, but needs regular renovation
  • Artificial Grass: 10-15 year lifespan before replacement needed

Best for UK Climate?

Natural turf thrives in the UK's temperate climate but struggles during droughts or heavy rain. Artificial grass performs consistently regardless of weather, making it ideal for high-traffic areas, shaded gardens, or families with children and pets.

Artificial Grass Specifications Explained

  • Pile Height: 25-35mm ideal for UK gardens (realistic look, comfortable feel)
  • Density: 16,000-18,000 stitches/m² for quality (denser = more realistic)
  • Material: Polyethylene (soft) or Polypropylene (durable, cheaper)
  • Backing: Dual-layer latex for stability and drainage
  • Drainage: Minimum 30 litres/m²/minute (UK rainfall requirement)
  • UV Resistance: Minimum 8,000 hours (prevents fading in sun)

Top UK Artificial Grass Brands (2025)

  • Namgrass: Premium brand, £60-£96/m², 10-year warranty, excellent realism
  • LazyLawn: Mid-range, £48-£72/m², 8-year warranty, good value
  • Easigrass: High-end, £72-£108/m², 12-year warranty, ultra-realistic
  • Marshalls: Budget-friendly, £36-£54/m², 5-year warranty, decent quality

Prices include supply only. Add £18-£30/m² for professional installation.

Installation Process (Professional vs DIY)

Professional Installation (Recommended)

  • 1. Excavate 75-100mm depth
  • 2. Install weed membrane
  • 3. Lay granite sub-base (compacted)
  • 4. Add sharp sand leveling layer
  • 5. Fit and secure artificial grass
  • 6. Infill with silica sand
  • Time: 2-3 days for typical garden

DIY Installation

  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Savings: £360-£720 (40m² garden)
  • Time: 2-3 weekends
  • Risk: Poor drainage if done incorrectly
  • Tools needed: Compactor, Stanley knife, joining tape
  • Professional recommended for drainage issues

Pet-Friendly Artificial Grass

If you have dogs, choose artificial grass with these features:

  • Enhanced drainage: 60+ litres/m²/minute (for urine runoff)
  • Antimicrobial backing: Prevents odor buildup
  • Durable yarn: Polypropylene blend resists wear from claws
  • Infill choice: Zeolite infill absorbs odors (not silica sand)
  • Maintenance: Hose down weekly, enzyme cleaner monthly

Common Problems & Solutions

  • Flattened pile: Brush with stiff broom monthly to restore bounce
  • Poor drainage: Install additional drainage holes in sub-base
  • Fading: Choose UV-resistant grass (8,000+ hours rating)
  • Weed growth: Ensure proper weed membrane installation
  • Joins visible: Professional installation avoids this issue
  • Heat retention: Hosing down cools surface on hot days

Warranty & Guarantees

Quality artificial grass comes with comprehensive warranties:

  • • UV Fade Protection: 8-12 years depending on brand
  • • Manufacturing Defects: 5-10 years
  • • Professional Installation Guarantee: Typically 2 years
  • • What's NOT covered: Improper maintenance, pet damage, fire

Environmental Considerations

Natural turf is biodegradable, supports wildlife, and absorbs carbon. Artificial grass is made from plastic but saves water and eliminates pesticide/fertilizer use. Both have environmental trade-offs.

Environmental Impact Comparison:

  • Water saved: 200-400 litres/week during summer (artificial grass)
  • CO₂ from lawnmower: Eliminated with artificial grass
  • Recyclability: Some brands now offer recycling programs
  • Lifespan disposal: Artificial grass goes to landfill after 15 years
